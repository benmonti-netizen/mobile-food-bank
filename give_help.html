Volunteering

Work directly with our staff and your community!

Another great way to give back, for those who are able, is to volunteer. While donations are essential to helping our organization thrive, volunteers are also necessary. Volunteering allows you to work directly with our staff and really see the effect your generosity has on the community. Whether monthly, weekly, or a few hours, whenever you can, any additional human resources are greatly appreciated and can make a massive difference as they help us give back more and faster. For those who wish to help, we offer a variety of times to work and various activities you can do, including preparation, packing, and distributing, allowing you to tailor your volunteering experience to what you want. Moreover, volunteering can also help you make more connections within your community and strengthen community bonds through collaborative efforts.

Donations

Every can donated is a mouth fed!

One way to help and give back to your community is through monetary or food donations. As a non-profit organization, we rely heavily on the generosity of donors and others in the community to give donations, empowering us to affect meaningful change within the community. Both food and monetary donations are appreciated as they allow our organization to provide food relief to more people in need and fund programs throughout the community. Donating is a great way to help, especially for those with busy schedules who do not have enough time to volunteer in person, and donations for non-profit organizations such as ours can be tax-deductible. Overall, food and monetary contributions are pivotal in empowering our organization to foster a better future for the community.
