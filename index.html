Our Mission

At the Mobile Food Bank, our mission is to provide convenient and accessible food security to families and individuals facing food insecurity, offering diverse and nutritious foods to promote healthy eating. We recognize that food insecurity can affect anyone, regardless of background or circumstance, and we are dedicated to ensuring that no one in our community goes hungry.
