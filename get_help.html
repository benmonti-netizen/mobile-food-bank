Do I Qualify?

To ensure that we get food to those who need it, we require that you qualify for food assistance by showing proof of financial need. We use a variety of criteria to determine and have specific requirements, all of which are listed below:

Income Level

A primary factor in determining the need for food assistance as we prioritize those with low-income levels who cannot afford to buy food themselves.

Residency

As a local food bank, we serve our community, and thus, you must live within that community to receive that aid.

Identification

We also require proof of identification via a driver's license or state ID.

Household Size

Along with residency, we also must know the size of your household to determine what kind of assistance is needed. This information includes other partners, children, or anyone else in the household utilizing these services.

Special Circumstances

Special circumstances are considered, including things such as medical bills and disabilities that could impact one's financial situation. If you do not need the income level requirement, but if these or other reasons still hinder your ability to have consistent access to food, you could benefit from our programs.

Limited Quantities

In addition to these requirements, we operate on a first-come, first-serve basis as we operate on limited resources. If you need assistance or have any questions about qualifying for assistance, don't hesitate to get in touch with us as soon as possible to ensure you get the help you or your family needs.

What if I Qualify?

Documentation

If you feel you qualify for our programs, send or drop off documents containing proof of income, residency, and identity, as well as any other relevant documents for us to verify. Click the button below to see examples of what documents cover what requriements.

Application

Once documents have been sent and verified, complete the form by clicking the button below. As an organization, we cater to your and your family's needs. Include any food allergies or other nutrition concerns so we can accomadate your needs!

Deliveries

Contents

When ordering a delivery, you can order a general delivery, which will contain nutrient-rich proteins, fruits, vegetables, and grains. It will also contain some shelf-stable beverages, such as apple juice and other smaller snacks. However, you can also specify certain foods when applying for a delivery or list any allergy/nutrition concerns so we can accommodate them!

Delivery Times

When applying for a delivery, choose a time during the day and a day of the week for us to deliver. If none of the offered times work for you, reach out and we will happily make any neccesary arrangements!

Noon: 12-3 AM
Evening: 5-7 AM
