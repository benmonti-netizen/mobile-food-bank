About Us

We are a small, local food bank dedicated to stopping food insecurity and hunger in our community one meal at a time. We were founded on the principle of compassion and commitment to help those in need, and together, we can make a great difference in our community and make it a better place for everyone. We also partner with larger organizations such as the Greater Pittburgh Community Food Bank and Feeding America.

FAQs

Q: What is a food bank?
A: A non-profit organization that collects, stores, and distributes food to those in need.

Q: Who has access to the services of a food bank?
A: We provide services to anyone facing food insecurity, meaning they consistently lack access to enough food.

Q: Does the food bank use COVID-19 measures?
A: We have implemented several safety measures, including masks, social distancing, and regularly sanitizing all services and volunteers' hands who handle any food items.

Donating / Volunteering

Q: Do I need to apply or qualify to drop off donations?
A: No, anyone and everyone is welcome to stop by anytime during our regular operating hours to drop off food donations unless they are large donations. In this case, we should be contacted in advance.

Q: What kinds of foods can I donate?
A: We accept all non-perishable items, such as canned and dry foods and shelf-stable beverages.

Q: What foods are most often needed?
A: Canned meat, fruits, vegetables, and pasta, as these are all items we try to include in food deliveries regularly.

Getting Help

Q: What documents do I need to know if I qualify for assistance?
A: These documents include proof of identification, residency, and income, all of which can be mailed in or dropped off in person. Click here to learn more.

Q: How often can you receive assistance from your food bank?
A: We will provide regular deliveries on a monthly or weekly basis, as specified.

Q: Can you help with dietary needs/restrictions?
A: Yes, if you tell us of any dietary needs/restrictions or allergies, we will ensure they are accommodated through deliveries and during prep.
