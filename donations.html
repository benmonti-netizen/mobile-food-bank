Donations

Food

Donations of non-perishable foods are pivotal to our services in preparing and delivering food to the community. These donations are essential because we rely primarily on food donations to prepare food deliveries for those in need. While we can purchase food if needed, that money is often required to cover operational costs for deliveries, food drives, or other programs we fund. Having enough food donations so we don't have to spend anything buying food makes running those programs easier. We accept donations from all sources, from individuals to businesses and community organizations, and often have food drives when we need specific items.

What Can I Donate?

YES

Non-Perishable Foods: Foods have a long shelf life and do not go bad easily:
Canned protiens, vetbales, fruits, soups, or beans

Boxed foods (cereal, pasta, rice)

Boxed meals (mac and cheese)

Peanut and other nut butters

Snacks (granola bars, crackers, trail mix)

Cooking oils and condiments

Tea, coffee, and hot chocolate mixes

Shelf-stable beverages (fruit juices)

NO

Unsealed packaging, even if it has since been resealed or appropriately stored since it was opened

Significantly damaged items (ripped boxes or seriously dented cans, for example)

Expired items (anything donated past its “use-by” or “sell-by” date)

Perishable items (meat or dairy, for example)

Homemade items, even if it has not been eaten and have been appropriately stored, or leftovers

Any items containing alcohol

Additionally, while we appreciate the generosity, we cannot accept any on-the-spot donations of large quantities or donations in bulk due to our limited space. If you wish to make a large donation, please contact us in advance to schedule a day and time to donate.

Monetary

While food donations are extremely important, monetary contributions are also very beneficial as there are numerous expenses we must pay for. Beyond buying additional food when necessary, your donations help to cover an array of operational costs, allowing us to do what we do. The expenses include our facility's constant maintenance and upkeep to ensure a safe, accessible, and clean environment for our staff and the food. Your donations can also help fund innovative programs that expand outreach to the community and help others facing food insecurity!
